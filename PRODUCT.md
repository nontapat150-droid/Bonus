# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Admins and Technicians managing inventory, oil records, and dispatching in both office and field environments.

## Product Purpose
A unified business suite to streamline operations, tracking high-value inventory (SN-based) and operational costs (oil/distance).

## Brand Personality
Professional, Efficient, Modern, Reliable.

## Anti-references
Cluttered legacy admin panels (like AdminLTE), heavy borders, and generic bootstrap-default styling.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over Complexity**: Dashboard metrics should be readable at a glance.
- **Mobile-First Utility**: Critical actions must be accessible for field technicians.
- **Subtle Elevation**: Use shadows and spacing instead of heavy borders to define hierarchy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for outdoor readability (technicians).
- Large touch targets for mobile use.
